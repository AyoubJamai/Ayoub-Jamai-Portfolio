HIYANYA PROJECT

Discover the documentary 'Hiyanya,' a profound exploration of an elderly woman's unwavering commitment to her ancestral land situated in the mountains, staunchly rejecting the prospect of residing with her sons due to her profound connection to the terrain. I am privileged to have served as the sound recordist and sound mixer for this project, contributing to the auditory dimensions that enrich the narrative. Through meticulous recording and precise mixing, 'Hiyanya' captures the essence of this compelling story, underscoring the profound ties between individuals and their land. .
